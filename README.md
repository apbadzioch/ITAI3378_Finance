# ITAI 3378 - Finance and Business
from syllabus

## Course Overview
The transformation in Finance and Business is a direct result of AI.
We will examine how AI is changing the business of Finance and 
financial tools. The impact of the changed in finance are having 
a dramatic effect in all business. We will also evaluate the new 
changes coming to the business of Finance. The profound effect of 
Generative AI is leading to new and clever ways to grow financial 
and business resources that are opening unanticipated opportunities 
to transform markets. AI is stimulating rare opportunities for 
unprecedented innovation and growth.

## Course Specific Student Learning Outcomes
1. Define key terminology in Finance, FinTech, digital currencies,
AI driven business intelligence, and financial automation systems.
2. Implement AI models for business applications such as financial
prediction, risk assessment, sentiment analysis, customer segmentation, 
and content generation using industry standard tools and platforms.
3. Interpret AI model outputs and business analytics to extract 
actionable insights that support strategic decision making and operational
improvements.
4. Critique the ethical considerations, regulatory requirements, and societal 
impacts of AI implementation in finance and business, including fairness, 
transparency, and accountability.
5. Construct and present a complete AI driven business solution that integrates 
multiple techniques and demonstrate workforce ready skills.
